All About Stephen

I'm Stephen Herd, a web developer with over 20 years of experience in development and more than 35 years of teaching. Early in my career, I worked as an automotive instructor at General Motors, where I taught advanced vehicle diagnostics and electronic systems courses. This experience ignited my passion for teaching, and I eventually earned a Master of Science in Computer Education and Cognitive Systems from the University of North Texas in 2006. After that, I transitioned to teaching Information Technology courses.

In addition to my teaching career, I've had the opportunity to build websites for non-profit organizations, helping them establish an online presence. I also provide professional development to higher education institutions, supporting faculty and staff in enhancing their technical skills. Currently, I teach Web Development at Dallas College, where I'm able to share my knowledge and help students develop the skills they need to succeed in the tech industry.

My background in both web development and instructional design allows me to blend technical expertise with effective teaching strategies. I'm passionate about helping students and organizations grow through technology, and I look forward to continuing to contribute to the field of education and web development.
